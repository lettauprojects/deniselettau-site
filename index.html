The attorney

How I got here

I did not set out to build a practice devoted to legacy and generational wealth. It began in the trust department of a regional bank, where I was a single parent looking for steady work and reliable hours. What I found there changed the course of my career.

I watched ordinary, hard-working families use careful planning to preserve everything they had built. I saw founders turn a single idea, and a great deal of risk, into companies and communities that outlived them. And I learned something that still shapes this firm: generational wealth is not reserved for the ultra-wealthy. It is created on purpose, through clarity, planning, and love for the people who come next.

That lesson drives my work every day. Everyone deserves the chance to protect what they have built, care for the people they love, and leave something meaningful behind. This firm exists to make that happen, with compassion, clarity, and a commitment to your future.

Without a plan, probate, frozen accounts, and family conflict become the default. Planning is simply choosing otherwise, on your own terms, while it is still yours to decide.

About us

Meet Denise

Denise Lettau is the founder of the Law Offices of Denise Lettau, P.A., a boutique law practice focused on holistic estate planning with an emphasis on aging, disability, and family legacy. She brings nearly fifteen years of experience in the banking sector, where she served as a trusted advisor to elderly and disabled clients at institutions including SunTrust and Wells Fargo. She later held leadership roles in the nonprofit and care management sectors before launching her own firm in 2024.

Fluent in German, Denise has a global perspective, having lived and taught in Germany following her work at the Institute of International Law in Kiel, Germany. She holds degrees from Pacific Lutheran University and Seattle University School of Law.

A committed community leader, she serves on several boards within the Jewish Federation of Broward County and co-founded the Alcee Hastings Black-Jewish Alliance. She also sits on the Board of the Area Agency on Aging of Broward County.

Denise is married and the proud mom of one son. Outside of work, she enjoys reading, traveling, and cheering for her favorite Formula 1 team.

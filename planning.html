There’s no better time than today.

Doing the right thing for yourself, and the people you love, should not have to wait. We help you understand your options, choose the right strategies, and see them through to completion.

01

Don’t leave a mess.

Will or trust, or both? What do you really need to keep your family out of court and out of conflict when something happens to you? We look at your family dynamics, and your assets, to ensure you understand what the state’s plan is for you, and then help you create the plan that is right for you, to achieve your desired outcomes for yourself and the people you love.

02

Protect who, and what, matters.

Don’t fail your loved ones. Even if you have created a plan already, it may not work when your family needs it. Estate plans, even with the very best documents, fail when they are not well counseled, not updated, and when your assets are not owned in the right way. Your family deserves more.

03

Have financial peace of mind.

It does not matter how much money you have. Estate planning is simply about attending to the inevitable, and bravely facing the end, in service to the best possible legal and financial choices throughout life. Our heart-centered counseling process ensures you make the right choices for yourself, and the people you love.
